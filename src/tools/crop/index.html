How to Crop Images

1. Upload Image

Drag and drop your image or click to browse. Supports JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP.

2. Select Area

Click and drag on the image to select the area you want to crop. Use handles to adjust the selection.

3. Adjust Settings

Choose aspect ratio or set custom dimensions. Maintain aspect ratio to avoid distortion.

4. Crop & Download

Click "Crop Image" to process, then download your cropped image.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 50MB per image.

Can I crop multiple images at once?

Currently, you can only crop one image at a time. Upload each image separately.

Is my image secure?

Yes! All image processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.
